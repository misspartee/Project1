The secret to creating the perfect interactive design lies within five steps. Step one design with simplicity. Pay attention to the colors, typography, and spacing you use for your interface. Step two design with mental triggers in mind. Understanding human psychology goes a long way when it comes to interactive designs. Knowing that people are drawn to what is relevant to them in their lives at the moment is important. Also, people like feeling in control. Step three connect emotionally with your users. Focus on the one emotion you want your design to convey. Step four, design for comfort and predictability. Think about thumb zone and ideal placement for primary navigation. Lastly, step five is remembering humans come first. Ask yourself questions like: is this personal? Is this intuitive? Do you feel like you are part of the design?
