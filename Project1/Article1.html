Designers have a vocabulary of their own. To list a few good vocab words every designer should know, I would start with balance. Balance is the placement of the elements on a page, making sure both graphics and text are evenly distributed. Having symmetry is a good way to achieve balance. Proximity is another good vocab word. This deals with the way in which design elements are grouped on a page. This can aid the user through the flow of your design. Next we have repetition. If executed correctly repetition maintains a unified look for your design. Another word on the vocab list is Mock up. Mock ups are real world or digital models that represent your design ideas.
