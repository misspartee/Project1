Color theory is both science and art. It deals with how humans perceive color. This is because colors can create subliminal messaging. Color is perception. People decide whether or not they like a product in 90 seconds or less. 90% of their decision is based solely on color. The additive color model is the mixing of light. This technique allows you to create colors by mixing red, green, and blue light sources. Another color model is the subtracting color model. Most people are more familiar with this color model because it's what we learned in Kindergarten when mixing paints. The most commonly known color model is the color wheel. The color wheel consist of primary colors, secondary colors, and tertiary colors. You can also identify warm colors and cool colors. The possibilities are endless.
